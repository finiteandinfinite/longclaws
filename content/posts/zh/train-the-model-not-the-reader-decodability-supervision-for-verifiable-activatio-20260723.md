# Train the Model, Not the Reader: Decodability Supervision for Verifiable Activation Explanations

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.20379v1)

作者: Hiskias Dingeto

The test is structurally insensitive to individual false claims: if flipping a claim does not change the reconstruction, the claim is never penalized.
