# EMO: Pretraining Mixture of Experts for Emergent Modularity

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06663v1)

作者: Ryan Wang, Akshita Bhagia, Sewon Min

Large language models are typically deployed as monolithic systems, requiring the full model even when applications need only a narrow subset of capabilities, e.g., code, math, or domain-specific knowledge. Mixture-of-Experts (MoEs) seemingly offer a potential alternative by activating only a subset of experts per input, but in practice, restricting inference to a subset of experts for a given domain leads to severe performance degradation.
