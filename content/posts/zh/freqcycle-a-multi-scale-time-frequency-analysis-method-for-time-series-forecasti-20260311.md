# FreqCycle: A Multi-Scale Time-Frequency Analysis Method for Time Series Forecasting

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09661v1)

作者: Boya Zhang, Shuaijie Yin, Huiwen Zhu 等

Mining time-frequency features is critical for time series forecasting. Existing research has predominantly focused on modeling low-frequency patterns, where most time series energy is concentrated. The overlooking of mid to high frequency continues to limit further performance gains in deep learning models.
