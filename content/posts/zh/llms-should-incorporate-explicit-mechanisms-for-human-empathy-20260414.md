# LLMs Should Incorporate Explicit Mechanisms for Human Empathy

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.10557v1)

作者: Xiaoxing You, Qiang Huang, Jun Yu

This paper argues that Large Language Models (LLMs) should incorporate explicit mechanisms for human empathy. As LLMs become increasingly deployed in high-stakes human-centered settings, their success depends not only on correctness or fluency but on faithful preservation of human perspectives. Yet, current LLMs systematically fail at this requirement: even when well-aligned and policy-compliant, they often attenuate affect, misrepresent contextual salience, and rigidify relational stance in way
