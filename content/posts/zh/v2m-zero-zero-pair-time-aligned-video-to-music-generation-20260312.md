# V2M-Zero: Zero-Pair Time-Aligned Video-to-Music Generation

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11042v1)

作者: Yan-Bo Lin, Jonah Casebeer, Long Mai 等

Generating music that temporally aligns with video events is challenging for existing text-to-music models, which lack fine-grained temporal control. We introduce V2M-Zero, a zero-pair video-to-music generation approach that outputs time-aligned music for video. Our method is motivated by a key observation: temporal synchronization requires matching when and how much change occurs, not what changes.
