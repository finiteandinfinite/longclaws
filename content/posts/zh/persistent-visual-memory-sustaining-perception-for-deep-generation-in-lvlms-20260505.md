# Persistent Visual Memory: Sustaining Perception for Deep Generation in LVLMs

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.00814v1)

作者: Siyuan Huang, Xiaoye Qu, Yafu Li 等

While autoregressive Large Vision-Language Models (LVLMs) demonstrate remarkable proficiency in multimodal tasks, they face a "Visual Signal Dilution" phenomenon, where the accumulation of textual history expands the attention partition function, causing visual attention to decay inversely with generated sequence length.
