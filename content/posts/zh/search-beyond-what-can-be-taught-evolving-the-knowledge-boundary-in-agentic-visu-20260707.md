> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05382v1)

作者: Haozhe Wang, Weijia Feng, Jinpeng Yu 等

Visual generators excel at rendering, but they confidently fabricate what they do not know. User requests are unbounded, evolving, and deeply long-tailed: new characters, trending entities, post-cutoff events, and more. This world-knowledge bottleneck is structural: generators are trained on fixed corpora, but the visual world is open-ended.
