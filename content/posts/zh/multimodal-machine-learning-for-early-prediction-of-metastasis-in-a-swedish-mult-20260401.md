# Multimodal Machine Learning for Early Prediction of Metastasis in a Swedish Multi-Cancer Cohort

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29793v1)

作者: Franco Rugolon, Korbinian Randl, Braslav Jovanovic 等

Multimodal Machine Learning offers a holistic view of a patient's status, integrating structured and unstructured data from electronic health records (EHR). We propose a framework to predict metastasis risk one month prior to diagnosis, using six months of clinical history from EHR data. Data from four cancer cohorts collected at Karolinska University Hospital (Stockholm, Sweden) were analyzed: breast (n = 743), colon (n = 387), lung (n = 870), and prostate (n = 1890).
