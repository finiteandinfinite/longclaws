# From Seeing to Thinking: Decoupling Perception and Reasoning Improves Post-Training of Vision-Language Models

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20177v1)

作者: Juncheng Wu, Hardy Chen, Haoqin Tu 等

Recent advances in vision-language models (VLMs) emphasize long chain-of-thought reasoning; yet, we find that their performance on visual tasks is primarily limited by a lack of visual perception as opposed to reasoning itself. In this work, we systematically study the interplay between perception and reasoning in VLM post-training by decomposing their capabilities into three separate training stages: visual perception, visual reasoning, and textual reasoning, incorporating specialized training
