作者: Alexey Potapov, Oleg Shcherbakov, Ivan Kravchenko

We evaluate JEPA-style predictive representation learning versus reconstruction-based autoencoders on a controlled "TV-series" linear dynamical system with known latent state and a single noise parameter. While an initial comparison suggests JEPA is markedly more robust to noise, further diagnostics show that autoencoder failures are strongly influenced by asymmetries in objectives and by bottleneck/component-selection effects (confirmed by PCA baselines).
