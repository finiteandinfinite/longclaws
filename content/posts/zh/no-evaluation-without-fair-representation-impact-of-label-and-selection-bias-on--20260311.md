# No evaluation without fair representation : Impact of label and selection bias on the evaluation, performance and mitigation of classification models

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09662v1)

作者: Magali Legast, Toon Calders, François Fouss

Bias can be introduced in diverse ways in machine learning datasets, for example via selection or label bias. Although these bias types in themselves have an influence on important aspects of fair machine learning, their different impact has been understudied. In this work, we empirically analyze the effect of label bias and several subtypes of selection bias on the evaluation of classification models, on their performance, and on the effectiveness of bias mitigation methods.
