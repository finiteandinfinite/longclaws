> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.06667v1)

作者: Omar El Khalifi, Thomas Rossi, Oscar Fossey 等

We present ActCam, a zero-shot method for video generation that jointly transfers character motion from a driving video into a new scene and enables per-frame control of intrinsic and extrinsic camera parameters.
