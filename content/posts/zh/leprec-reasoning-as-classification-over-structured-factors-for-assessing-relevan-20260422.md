> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.19464v1)

作者: Fanyu Wang, Xiaoxi Kang, Paul Burgess 等

More than half of the global population struggles to meet their civil justice needs due to limited legal resources. While Large Language Models (LLMs) have demonstrated impressive reasoning capabilities, significant challenges remain even at the foundational step of legal issue identification.
