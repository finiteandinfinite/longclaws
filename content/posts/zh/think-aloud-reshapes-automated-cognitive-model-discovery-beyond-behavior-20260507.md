作者: Hanbo Xie, Akshay K. Jagadish, Lan Pan 等

However, it is well-known that models produced from the behavioral trajectory alone are typically under-determined. In this work, we explore the use of Think Aloud traces as an additional form of data constraint during automated model discovery.
