# Select to Think: Unlocking SLM Potential with Local Sufficiency

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.26940v1)

作者: Wenxuan Ye, Yangyang Zhang, Xueli An 等

Small language models (SLMs) offer computational efficiency for scalable deployment, yet they often fall short of the reasoning power exhibited by their larger counterparts (LLMs). To mitigate this gap, current approaches invoke an LLM to generate tokens at points of reasoning divergence, but these external calls introduce substantial latency and costs. Alternatively, standard distillation is often hindered by the capacity limitation, as SLMs struggle to accurately mimic the LLM's complex genera
