> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14137v1)

作者: Itay Itzhak, Eliya Habba, Gabriel Stanovsky 等

Evaluating LLMs is challenging, as benchmark scores often fail to capture models' real-world usefulness. Instead, users often rely on ``vibe-testing'': informal experience-based evaluation, such as comparing models on coding tasks related to their own workflow. While prevalent, vibe-testing is often too ad hoc and unstructured to analyze or reproduce at scale.
