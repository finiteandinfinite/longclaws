> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20824v1)

作者: Ana Sanchez-Fernandez, Thomas Pinetz, Werner Zellinger 等

The central problem in biomedical imaging are batch effects: systematic technical variations unrelated to the biological signal of interest. These batch effects critically undermine experimental reproducibility and are the primary cause of failure of deep learning systems on new experimental batches, preventing their practical use in the real world.
