# The Value Axis: Language Models Encode Whether They're on the Right Track

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17056v1)

作者: Nick Jiang, Isaac Kauvar, Jack Lindsey

We investigate whether language models internally track the value of their current trajectory, defined as the likelihood that their ongoing strategy will achieve their goals. Using synthetic, in-context reinforcement learning data, we construct a "value" axis for Qwen3-8B. We find that activations along this axis distinguish between high vs. low verbalized confidence, rollouts without and with backtracking, and correct vs. corrupted code.
