# Structured Latent Dynamics in Wireless CSI via Homomorphic World Models

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20048v1)

作者: Salmane Naoumi, Mehdi Bennis, Marwa Chafii

We introduce a self-supervised framework for learning predictive and structured representations of wireless channels by modeling the temporal evolution of channel state information (CSI) in a compact latent space. Our method casts the problem as a world modeling task and leverages the Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture (JEPA) to learn action-conditioned latent dynamics from CSI trajectories.
