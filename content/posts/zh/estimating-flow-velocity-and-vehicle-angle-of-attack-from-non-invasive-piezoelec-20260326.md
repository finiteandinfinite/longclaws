# Estimating Flow Velocity and Vehicle Angle-of-Attack from Non-invasive Piezoelectric Structural Measurements Using Deep Learning

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.23496v1)

作者: Chandler B. Smith, S. Hales Swift, Andrew Steyer 等

Accurate estimation of aerodynamic state variables such as freestream velocity and angle of attack (AoA) is important for aerodynamic load prediction, flight control, and model validation. This work presents a non-intrusive method for estimating vehicle velocity and AoA from structural vibration measurements rather than direct flow instrumentation such as pitot tubes.
