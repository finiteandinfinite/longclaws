作者: Shriram Chennakesavalu, Kirill Shmilovich, Hayley Weir 等

Large Language Models (LLMs) have the potential to accelerate small molecule drug design due to their ability to reason about information from diverse sources and formats. However, their practical utility remains unclear due to the lack of benchmarks that reflect real-world scenarios.
