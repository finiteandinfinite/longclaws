# Do AI Agents Know When a Task Is Simple? Toward Complexity-Aware Reasoning and Execution

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13034v1)

作者: Junjie Yin, Xinyu Feng

Large language model (LLM) agents increasingly automate multi-step engineering and informatics workflows, yet they rarely ask how much effort a task actually requires. They often follow a maximum-context-first strategy--re-reading files and dependencies they have already seen--turning a one-line edit into a small code-base audit.
