# Beyond Text Following: Repairable Arbitration Reversals in Audio-Language Models

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05161v1)

作者: Yichen Gao, Yiqun Zhang, Zijing Wang 等

Audio-language models (ALMs) often follow text that conflicts with audio, even when the audio evidence is clear. This raises a basic question: is the audio-supported answer unavailable, or is it represented but overridden by the conflicting text? We examine this question using a same-audio counterfactual that keeps the audio fixed, removes only the conflicting text, and measures the resulting shift in model preference.
