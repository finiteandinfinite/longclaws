> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.18746v1)

作者: Yining Hong, Jiageng Liu, Han Yin 等

Spatial intelligence unfolds through a perception-action loop: agents act to acquire observations, and reason about how observations vary as a function of action. Rather than passively processing what is seen, they actively uncover what is unseen - occluded structure, dynamics, containment, and functionality that cannot be resolved from passive sensing alone.
