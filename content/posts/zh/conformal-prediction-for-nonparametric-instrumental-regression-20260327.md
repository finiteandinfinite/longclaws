# Conformal Prediction for Nonparametric Instrumental Regression

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25509v1)

作者: Masahiro Kato

We propose a method for constructing distribution-free prediction intervals in nonparametric instrumental variable regression (NPIV), with finite-sample coverage guarantees. Building on the conditional guarantee framework in conformal inference, we reformulate conditional coverage as marginal coverage over a class of IV shifts $\mathcal{F}$.
