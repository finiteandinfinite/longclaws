# Understanding Truncated Positional Encodings for Graph Neural Networks

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.13671v1)

作者: James Flora, Mitchell Black, Weng-Keen Wong 等

Positional encodings (PEs) enhance the power of graph neural networks (GNNs), both theoretically and empirically. Two of the most popular families of PEs - spectral (e.g., Laplacian eigenspaces, effective resistance) and walk-based (polynomials of the adjacency matrix) - are theoretically equivalent in expressive power, with expressivity between the 1-WL and 3-WL tests.
