# Online Safety Monitoring for LLMs

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02510v1)

作者: Mona Schirmer, Metod Jazbec, Alexander Timans 等

Despite alignment training, LLMs remain prone to generating unsafe outputs at deployment time. Monitoring outputs online and raising an alarm when safety can no longer be assumed is therefore critical. We study a simple real-time monitor that turns a verifier signal from an external model into an alarm decision by thresholding, with the threshold calibrated via risk control.
