# Understanding and Improving Continuous Adversarial Training for LLMs via In-context Learning Theory

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.12817v1)

作者: Shaopeng Fu, Di Wang

Adversarial training (AT) is an effective defense for large language models (LLMs) against jailbreak attacks, but performing AT on LLMs is costly. To improve the efficiency of AT for LLMs, recent studies propose continuous AT (CAT) that searches for adversarial inputs within the continuous embedding space of LLMs during AT.
