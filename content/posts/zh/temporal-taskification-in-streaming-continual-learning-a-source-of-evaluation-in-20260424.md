作者: Nicolae Filat, Ahmed Hussain, Konstantinos Kalogiannis 等

We argue that this temporal taskification step is not a neutral preprocessing choice, but a structural component of evaluation: different valid splits of the same stream can induce different CL regimes and therefore different benchmark conclusions.
