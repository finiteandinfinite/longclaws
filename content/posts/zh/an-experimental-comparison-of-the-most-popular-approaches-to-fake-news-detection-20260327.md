# An Experimental Comparison of the Most Popular Approaches to Fake News Detection

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25501v1)

作者: Pietro Dell'Oglio, Alessandro Bondielli, Francesco Marcelloni 等

In recent years, fake news detection has received increasing attention in public debate and scientific research. Despite advances in detection techniques, the production and spread of false information have become more sophisticated, driven by Large Language Models (LLMs) and the amplification power of social media. We present a critical assessment of 12 representative fake news detection approaches, spanning traditional machine learning, deep learning, transformers, and specialized cross-domain
