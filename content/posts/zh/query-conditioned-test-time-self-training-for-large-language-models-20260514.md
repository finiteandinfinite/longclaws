作者: Chaehee Song, Minseok Seo, Yeeun Seong 等

Large language models (LLMs) are typically deployed with fixed parameters, and their performance is often improved by allocating more computation at inference time. While such test-time scaling can be effective, it cannot correct model misconceptions or adapt the model to the specific structure of an individual query.
