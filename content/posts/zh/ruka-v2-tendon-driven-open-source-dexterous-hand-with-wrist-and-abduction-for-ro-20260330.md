# Ruka-v2: Tendon Driven Open-Source Dexterous Hand with Wrist and Abduction for Robot Learning

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.26660v1)

作者: Xinqi, Liu, Ruoxi Hu 等

Lack of accessible and dexterous robot hardware has been a significant bottleneck to achieving human-level dexterity in robots. Last year, we released Ruka, a fully open-sourced, tendon-driven humanoid hand with 11 degrees of freedom - 2 per finger and 3 at the thumb - buildable for under $1,300. It was one of the first fully open-sourced humanoid hands, and introduced a novel data-driven approach to finger control that captures tendon dynamics within the control system.
