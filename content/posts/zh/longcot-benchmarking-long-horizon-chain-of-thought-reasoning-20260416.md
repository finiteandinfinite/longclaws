# LongCoT: Benchmarking Long-Horizon Chain-of-Thought Reasoning

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14140v1)

作者: Sumeet Ramesh Motwani, Daniel Nichols, Charles London 等

As language models are increasingly deployed for complex autonomous tasks, their ability to reason accurately over longer horizons becomes critical. An essential component of this ability is planning and managing a long, complex chain-of-thought (CoT). We introduce LongCoT, a scalable benchmark of 2,500 expert-designed problems spanning chemistry, mathematics, computer science, chess, and logic to isolate and directly measure the long-horizon CoT reasoning capabilities of frontier models.
