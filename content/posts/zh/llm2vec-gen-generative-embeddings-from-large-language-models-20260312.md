作者: Parishad BehnamGhader, Vaibhav Adlakha, Fabian David Schmidt 等

LLM-based text embedders typically encode the semantic content of their input. However, embedding tasks require mapping diverse inputs to similar outputs. Typically, this input-output is addressed by training embedding models with paired data using contrastive learning.
