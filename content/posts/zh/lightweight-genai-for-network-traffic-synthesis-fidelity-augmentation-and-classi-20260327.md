作者: Giampaolo Bovenzi, Domenico Ciuonzo, Jonatan Krolikowski 等

While Network Traffic Generation (NTG) provides an effective means to mitigate data scarcity, conventional generative methods struggle to model the complex temporal dynamics of modern traffic or/and often incur significant computational cost.
