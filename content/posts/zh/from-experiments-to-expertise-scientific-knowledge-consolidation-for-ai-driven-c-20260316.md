> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.13191v1)

作者: Haonan Huang

What distinguishes research from routine execution is the progressive accumulation of knowledge -- learning which approaches fail, recognizing patterns across systems, and applying understanding to new problems.
