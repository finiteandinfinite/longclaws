# New Bounds for the Last Iterate of the Stochastic subGradient Method

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.24879v1)

作者: Guglielmo Beretta, Tommaso Cesari, Roberto Colomboni 等

We study the last iterate of the stochastic subgradient method for one-dimensional convex Lipschitz objectives. For a fixed horizon $n$, we consider the standard fixed stepsizes $η=Θ(1/\sqrt n)$. We prove that, for such stepsize policies, under additive i.i.d. subgradient noise with uniformly bounded variance, the last iterate features an optimization error of order $1/\sqrt n$, thereby removing the extra $(\log n)$ factor present in existing generic bounds.
