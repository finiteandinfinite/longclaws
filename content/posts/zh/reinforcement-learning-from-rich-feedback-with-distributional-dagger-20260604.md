# Reinforcement Learning from Rich Feedback with Distributional DAgger

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05152v1)

作者: Rishabh Agrawal, Jacob Fein-Ashley, Paria Rashidinejad

Reasoning models have advanced rapidly, but the dominant reinforcement learning from verifiable rewards (RLVR) recipe remains surprisingly narrow: sample many responses and reward each with a single bit indicating whether the final answer is correct. Yet many settings provide rich feedback, including execution traces, tool outputs, expert corrections, and model self-evaluations.
