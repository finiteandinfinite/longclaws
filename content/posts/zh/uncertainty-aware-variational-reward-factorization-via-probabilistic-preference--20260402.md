作者: Gyuseok Lee, Wonbin Kweon, Zhenrui Yue 等

Reward factorization personalizes large language models (LLMs) by decomposing rewards into shared basis functions and user-specific weights. Yet, existing methods estimate user weights from scarce data in isolation and as deterministic points, leading to inaccurate and unreliable inference. We introduce Variational Reward Factorization (VRF), an uncertainty-aware framework that represents each user's preferences as a variational distribution in a shared preference space.
