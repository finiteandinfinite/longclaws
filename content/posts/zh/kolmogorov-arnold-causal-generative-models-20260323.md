# Kolmogorov-Arnold causal generative models

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.20184v1)

作者: Alejandro Almodóvar, Mar Elizo, Patricia A. Apellániz 等

Causal generative models provide a principled framework for answering observational, interventional, and counterfactual queries from observational data. However, many deep causal models rely on highly expressive architectures with opaque mechanisms, limiting auditability in high-stakes domains. We propose KaCGM, a causal generative model for mixed-type tabular data where each structural equation is parameterized by a Kolmogorov--Arnold Network (KAN).
