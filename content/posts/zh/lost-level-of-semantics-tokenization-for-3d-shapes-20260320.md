# LoST: Level of Semantics Tokenization for 3D Shapes

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.17995v1)

作者: Niladri Shekhar Dutt, Zifan Shi, Paul Guerrero 等

Tokenization is a fundamental technique in the generative modeling of various modalities. In particular, it plays a critical role in autoregressive (AR) models, which have recently emerged as a compelling option for 3D generation. However, optimal tokenization of 3D shapes remains an open question. State-of-the-art (SOTA) methods primarily rely on geometric level-of-detail (LoD) hierarchies, originally designed for rendering and compression.
