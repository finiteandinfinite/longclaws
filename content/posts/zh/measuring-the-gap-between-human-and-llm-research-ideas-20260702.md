# Measuring the Gap Between Human and LLM Research Ideas

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.01233v1)

作者: Ziyu Chen, Yilun Zhao, Arman Cohan

LLMs are increasingly used to brainstorm research ideas, but existing evaluations mostly judge individual ideas by novelty, feasibility, or expert preference. We instead ask: how far are current LLM-generated ideas from human researchers? To characterize this gap, we build a large-scale evaluation framework for ideation from high-quality human research papers. For each paper, we reverse-engineer a small set of closely related prior works that likely inspired its core idea. LLMs are then prompted
