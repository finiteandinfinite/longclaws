# Intersectional Fairness in Large Language Models

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20677v1)

作者: Chaima Boufaied, Ronnie De Souza Santos, Ann Barcomb

Large Language Models (LLMs) are increasingly deployed in socially sensitive settings, raising concerns about fairness and biases, particularly across intersectional demographic attributes. In this paper, we systematically evaluate intersectional fairness in six LLMs using ambiguous and disambiguated contexts from two benchmark datasets.
