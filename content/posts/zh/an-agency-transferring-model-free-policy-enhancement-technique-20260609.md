# An Agency-Transferring Model-Free Policy Enhancement Technique

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.09825v1)

作者: Anton Bolychev, Georgiy Malaniya, Sinan Ibrahim 等

Training reinforcement learning (RL) policies from scratch is costly: it requires careful reward and environment design, extensive tuning, and substantial computation. Yet many control problems already have a functional but suboptimal policy available as a baseline. This paper proposes a method for embedding such a baseline into the RL training process, simultaneously improving training efficiency relative to from-scratch methods and producing a learning policy that outperforms the baseline.
