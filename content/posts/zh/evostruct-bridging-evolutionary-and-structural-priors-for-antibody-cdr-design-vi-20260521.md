作者: Mansoor Ahmed, Sujin Lee, Umar Khayaz 等

Equivariant graph neural network (GNN) methods for antibody complementarity-determining region (CDR) design achieve the highest sequence recovery but suffer from severe vocabulary collapse. The current best GNN methods over-predict very few amino acids, such as tyrosine and glycine, while ignoring functionally important residues.
