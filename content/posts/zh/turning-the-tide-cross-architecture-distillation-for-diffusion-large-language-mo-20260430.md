# Turning the TIDE: Cross-Architecture Distillation for Diffusion Large Language Models

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.26951v1)

作者: Gongbo Zhang, Wen Wang, Ye Tian 等

Diffusion large language models (dLLMs) offer parallel decoding and bidirectional context, but state-of-the-art dLLMs require billions of parameters for competitive performance. While existing distillation methods for dLLMs reduce inference steps within a single architecture, none address cross-architecture knowledge transfer, in which the teacher and student differ in architecture, attention mechanism, and tokenizer.
