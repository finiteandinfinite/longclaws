# How reliable are LLMs when it comes to playing dice?

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07515v1)

作者: Luca Avena, Gianmarco Bet, Bernardo Busoni

We investigate the probabilistic reasoning capabilities of large language models through a controlled benchmarking study on discrete probability problems. We constructed two datasets, respectively a set of standard exercises and a set of counterintuitive exercises, designed to trigger heuristic reasoning, and evaluated 8 state-of-the-art models, each tested with and without Chain-of-Thought prompting.
