# The Geometry of Forgetting: Temporal Knowledge Drift as an Independent Axis in LLM Representations

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.09195v1)

作者: Rania Elbadry, Ahmed Heakl, Fan Zhang 等

Large language models confidently produce outdated answers, and no existing method can detect them. We show this is not an engineering failure but a structural one: temporal drift, whether a stored fact has changed since training, is encoded as a direction in the residual stream geometrically orthogonal to both correctness and uncertainty.
