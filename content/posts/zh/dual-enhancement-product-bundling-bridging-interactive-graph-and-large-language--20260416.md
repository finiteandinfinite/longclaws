# Dual-Enhancement Product Bundling: Bridging Interactive Graph and Large Language Model

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14030v1)

作者: Zhe Huang, Peng Wang, Yan Zheng 等

Product bundling boosts e-commerce revenue by recommending complementary item combinations. However, existing methods face two critical challenges: (1) collaborative filtering approaches struggle with cold-start items owing to dependency on historical interactions, and (2) LLMs lack inherent capability to model interactive graph directly. To bridge this gap, we propose a dual-enhancement method that integrates interactive graph learning and LLM-based semantic understanding for product bundling.
