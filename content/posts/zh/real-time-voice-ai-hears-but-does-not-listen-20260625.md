# Real-Time Voice AI Hears but Does Not Listen

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26083v1)

作者: Martijn Bartelds, Federico Bianchi, James Zou

Speech conveys information through both words and vocal delivery. We evaluate four leading production realtime voice systems-OpenAI's GPT Realtime 2, Google's Gemini 3.1 Flash Live, and Alibaba's Qwen3.5 Omni Plus and Omni Flash-on tasks where the words and the delivery patterns both convey meaningful information.
