# Storm Surge Modeling, Bias Correction, Graph Neural Networks, Graph Convolution Networks

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.20688v1)

作者: Noujoud Nader, Stefanos Giaremis, Clint Dawson 等

Storm surge forecasting remains a critical challenge in mitigating the impacts of tropical cyclones on coastal regions, particularly given recent trends of rapid intensification and increasing nearshore storm activity. Traditional high fidelity numerical models such as ADCIRC, while robust, are often hindered by inevitable uncertainties arising from various sources.
