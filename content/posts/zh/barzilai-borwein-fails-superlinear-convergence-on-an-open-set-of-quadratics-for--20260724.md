# Barzilai-Borwein Fails Superlinear Convergence on an Open Set of Quadratics for Every Dimension $n\geq 4$

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.21579v1)

作者: Dawei Li, Xiaotian Jiang, Mingyi Hong

Barzilai--Borwein (BB) method has shown strong practical performance in continuous optimization, yet its convergence dynamics remains poorly understood. In particular, a central unresolved question is whether BB converges superlinearly for almost every strictly convex quadratic problem and initialization.
