> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.15306v1)

作者: Yao Tong, Jiayuan Ye, Anastasia Borovykh 等

Whether language models can systematically generalize remains actively debated. Yet empirical performance is jointly shaped by multiple factors such as training data, training paradigms, and inference-time strategies, making failures difficult to interpret.
