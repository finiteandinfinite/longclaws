# Rhetorical Questions in LLM Representations: A Linear Probing Study

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.14128v1)

作者: Louie Hong Yao, Vishesh Anand, Yuan Zhuang 等

Rhetorical questions are asked not to seek information but to persuade or signal stance. How large language models internally represent them remains unclear. We analyze rhetorical questions in LLM representations using linear probes on two social-media datasets with different discourse contexts, and find that rhetorical signals emerge early and are most stably captured by last-token representations.
