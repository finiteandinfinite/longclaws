# Geometry-Guided Camera Motion Understanding in VideoLLMs

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.13119v1)

作者: Haoan Feng, Sri Harsha Musunuri, Guan-Ming Su

Camera motion is a fundamental geometric signal that shapes visual perception and cinematic style, yet current video-capable vision-language models (VideoLLMs) rarely represent it explicitly and often fail on fine-grained motion primitives. We address this gap with a framework of $\textbf{benchmarking}$, $\textbf{diagnosis}$, and $\textbf{injection}$.
