# Covertly improving intelligibility with data-driven adaptations of speech timing

> 来源: [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.30032v1)

作者: Paige Tuttösí, Angelica Lim, H. Henny Yeung 等

Human talkers often address listeners with language-comprehension challenges, such as hard-of-hearing or non-native adults, by globally slowing down their speech. However, it remains unclear whether this strategy actually makes speech more intelligible. Here, we take advantage of recent advancements in machine-generated speech allowing more precise control of speech rate in order to systematically examine how targeted speech-rate adjustments may improve comprehension.
